Commercial Harvesting Benefits

Commercial installations capture large volumes of stormwater, reducing burden on municipal drainage and cutting potable water costs by up to 40%. Systems range from 10 m³ underground cisterns to 200 m³ detention basins.

Integrated with smart monitoring, these solutions deliver real‑time data on inflow, storage levels, and water quality—essential for campuses, shopping malls, and industrial parks.

Commercial Techniques

Subsurface Detention & Recharge

Large‑scale modular chambers store peak flows underground, slowly infiltrating into the aquifer and preventing surface flooding.

Smart Filtration & Quality Monitoring

Automated cartridge filters and real‑time turbidity sensors ensure water meets reuse standards for landscaping and toilet flushing.

Hybrid Stormwater Management

Integrated bioswales, permeable paving, and cisterns deliver >90% retention of the 1‑year, 24‑hour design storm.

Installation & Maintenance

  • Inspect sediment traps and filters quarterly.
  • Verify sensor calibration every 6 months.
  • Flush detention chambers annually to remove silt.
  • Service pumps and valves before monsoon season.
